Pokhara Tour

Overview

Pokhara, a scenic city nestled in the foothills of the Himalayas, offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, adventure, and culture. Famous for its serene Phewa Lake, stunning mountain views, and thrilling activities like paragliding and trekking, Pokhara is an ideal destination for both relaxation and adventure. Visitors can explore iconic spots like Sarangkot for sunrise views, the Peace Pagoda, Davis Falls, and the International Mountain Museum. With a laid-back vibe, rich cultural heritage, and countless outdoor activities, a Pokhara tour promises a memorable experience for all types of travelers.

Day 1: Arrival & Lakeside Exploration

  • Arrive in Pokhara, check-in, and enjoy breakfast by the lake.
  • Boat ride on Phewa Lake, visit Tal Barahi Temple.
  • Stroll around Lakeside, shopping, and dinner with a sunset view.
  • EDinner at a lakeside restaurant with a view of the sunset over the lake.

Day 2: Adventure & Scenic Views

  • Early morning drive or hike to Sarangkot for a breathtaking sunrise view over the Annapurna mountain range.
  • Adventure activities (paragliding, zip-lining), visit the International Mountain Museum.
  • Visit Davis Falls & Gupteshwor Cave, dinner with Nepali cuisine.

Day 3: Trekking & Peaceful Views

  • Half-day trek to Poon Hill for Himalayan views.
  • Relax at a café, visit Peace Pagoda.
  • Unwind by the lakeside, dinner with live music.

Day 4: Culture & Local Exploration

  • Visit Bindhyabasini Temple and Seti River Gorge.
  • Explore Tibetan Refugee Camp and Pokhara Regional Museum.
  • Stroll through Prithvi Narayan Park, final dinner by the lake.
